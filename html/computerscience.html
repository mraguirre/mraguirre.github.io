About

At SGMS we make every effort to inspire students to make the dream of COLLEGE and CAREER READINESS a reality. We promote programs which help students achieve those goals. Most recently SGMS has partnered with code.org to present Computer Science Discoveries course to students in grades 7 - 8. This course will guide students through fundamental CS concepts (i.e. building websites, developing apps, working with physical circuit boards, etc...) which are becoming crucial tools for making sense of the modern world.
